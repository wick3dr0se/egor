# Egor Ferrismark Demo

![Ferris](/demos/ferrismark/assets/ferris_smol.png) (the little dude seen below)

A simple stress test for **Egor**, rendering tens to hundreds of thousands of Ferris crabs at stable FPS

![Ferrismark Screenshot](/media/ferrismark.png)

> Stable 55 FPS with ~160k crabs (Ryzen 5 2600 + Radeon PRO WX2100, Wayland/Arch Linux)

Just left click to spawn 999 crabs as many times as you can
